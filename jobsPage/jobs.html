Why You Should Join

Joining the Jī lóng family is a great idea for anyone trying to find a good job to work at. There are many benefits to joining us, we have pay starting at $18/per hour, free gifts, free meals, and so much more. We would love to have you join us!

History

Ji lóng was a family food resteraunt created during 1996 but we quickly grew into a nation wide food chain due to our success from cooking traditional chinese foods with an American twist on them. Now you can join our evergrowing family by joining us!
